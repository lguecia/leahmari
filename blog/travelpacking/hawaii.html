So you can imagine I was this traveler's first call when she found out she had to pack two weeks of clothes in a carry on.

Or maybe you just don't want to check a bag for your longer trips. Either way, I got you covered! Here is how we packed for two weeks in a warm weather destination in just one carry on suitcase.
