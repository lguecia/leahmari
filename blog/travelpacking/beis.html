All About That | Beis



I searched high and low for the perfect weekender bag, and what I came out with was the Beis Mini Weekender.


I hate sounding salesy, but I just might. These are the features I like:

  • The bottom of the bag acts like a travel cube and is removable to make a smaller bag for shorter trips with less items to pack

  • The main part of the bag is structured enough to act like a travel cube as well at the base level (and you can pile things on top)

  • There's a removable laptop case to keep your electronics safe

  • And an extra cross body strap for more carrying support

  • The bag isn't larger than my entire body, like the standard weekender is
